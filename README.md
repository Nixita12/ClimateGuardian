# ClimateGuardian
Climate change is one of the major environmental challenges affecting ecosystems and human life worldwide.

Greenhouse gases such as CO₂, methane, and nitrous oxide are increasing due to industrialization, transportation, energy consumption, and population growth.

These emissions lead to rising global temperatures, extreme weather events, melting glaciers, and sea level rise.

The United Nations introduced Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action to address climate change and promote environmental sustainability.

This project, ClimateGuardian AI, uses machine learning to analyze climate data, identify pollution sources, and predict future climate risks.

The system helps provide insights and recommendations that support effective climate action and sustainable environmental practices.
